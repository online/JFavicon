JFavicon

A small library for manipulating the favicon

JFavicon can dynamically change the favicon, adding to it a block with a counter (or changing your favicon to another, depending on the value of the counter).

This feature is very convenient for users and allow them to see updates on your page.

Demo

Look up and check out the favicon on this page to see the library in action on chrome and firefox.
